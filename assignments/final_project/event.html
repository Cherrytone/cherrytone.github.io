Cost

Entire Tour: $6,700

Single Week: $1,200

  • The entry fee includes
  • Accommodation is based on camp sites and motels ( minimal 17nights ) during the tour
  • One night hotel accommodation before the start in Sycamore and after the finish in Waynesville
  • Breakfast, lunch and meal at arrival ( during cycling days )
  • Fruit, water, sport drinks and energy bars ( during cycling days )
  • Dinner during cycling days if we are camping
  • Transportation of luggage
  • Organization of the journey
  • Support by bike mechanic
  • “Cycle the Continental Divide” cycling jersey
  • Full set of maps of the Adventure Cycling Association
  • Tour guide with information about the route
  • DVD with compilation of the best pictures
  • Communal transport between the finish of Cycle the Continental Divide in Waynesville and the hotel in Bartting
  • The entry fee does NOT include:
  • Airfares and transfer to the start in Sycamore and from the hotel in Bartting
  • Food and drinks on rest days
  • Dinner at the days with hotel or motel accommodation
  • Personal health- and travel-insurance
  • Entry fees of tourist sites

Requirements

The tour is open for men and women from all around the world with an age between 18 and 65.
Anyone in good health, a passion for cycling, and loves Adventure with a capital "A" is capable of cycling The Continental Divide. However, be ready for some long stages and tough riding conditions.
